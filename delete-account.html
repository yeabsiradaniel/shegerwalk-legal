How to request account deletion

To delete your ShegerWalk account and all associated data, send an email to yeabsirad9@gmail.com from the email address or phone number registered to your account.

Include "Delete Account Request" in the subject line. We will process your request within 30 days.

What data is deleted

  • Account profile (name, phone number, email)
  • Walking history and step data
  • Wallet balance and transaction history
  • Friends and social connections
  • FCM device tokens

Data we keep

We may retain minimal records required by law or for fraud prevention for up to 90 days after account deletion.
