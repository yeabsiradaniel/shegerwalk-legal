Delete Your ShegerWalk Account

Last updated: July 23, 2026

Delete instantly in the app (recommended)

You can permanently delete your account directly in the ShegerWalk app:

  1. Open ShegerWalk and go to Profile.
  2. Open Settings.
  3. Tap Delete Account and follow the confirmation steps.

Deletion is immediate and permanent. It cannot be undone. If you create a new account afterwards with the same phone number or email, it starts completely fresh — none of your previous data carries over.

What data is deleted

  • Account profile (name, phone number, email, profile picture)
  • Walking history, step data, and recorded GPS routes
  • Wallet balance and transaction history
  • Streaks, milestones, and challenge entries
  • Friends and social connections
  • Referral and notification data

Delete by email (alternative)

If you can't access the app, send an email to yeabsirad9@gmail.com from the email address or phone number registered to your account.

Include "Delete Account Request" in the subject line. We will process your request within 30 days.
