ShegerWalk Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026

Information We Collect

ShegerWalk collects health and fitness data (such as step count) to track your walking progress and calculate rewards. We also collect your phone number, name, and profile information when you create an account.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide the move-to-earn service, process rewards, send notifications, and improve the app.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with service providers who help us operate the app.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at: support@shegerwalk.app
